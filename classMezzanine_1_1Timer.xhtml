enum Mezzanine::Timer::TimerType

The style of timer to be used.

Enumerator
Normal 

Counts up forever, ignoring any goal and autoreset.

StopWatch 

Counts down, respecting any goal and autoreset.

Alarm 

Counts up, respecting any goal and autoreset.
