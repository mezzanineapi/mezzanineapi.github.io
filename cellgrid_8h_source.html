56  /// @brief This is a widget used to determine spacing and ordering of other widgets(Cells) on a grid like surface.
57  /// @details This grid does support cells of different sizes, but all the cells of the same row/column will behave
58  /// as if they share the size as the largest cell, in terms of spacing. The cells themselves will not be altered in
59  /// size, and only the appropriate dimention will be applied(I.E. Each column will be spaced according to the largest
60  /// width+spacing, and each row will be spaced according to the largest height+spacing). Thus it is ideal to have
61  /// each cell be the same size, but not required.
62  ///////////////////////////////////////
